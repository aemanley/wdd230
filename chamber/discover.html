Discover San Pedro Sula, Cortes

San Pedro Sula is a city with a wide cultural heritage. It is a city of happy and jovial people, a city full of contrasts, flavors, colors and a lot of love for culture. It is the second largest city in the Central American state of Honduras, located in the wide area of the isthmus that joins North and South America.

The population of the municipality is 777,877 people which is made up of 369,197 men and 408,680 women.
